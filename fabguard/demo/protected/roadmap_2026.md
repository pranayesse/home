# FabCorp Technology Roadmap 2026 — COMPANY CONFIDENTIAL

Internal only. Do not distribute.

## DRAM

The 1-gamma node enters risk production in Q2 2026. Yield ramp is gated
on the revised litho recipe (v14) and the tightened overlay budget on the
storage node mask. Tape-out of the VOLTA-9 compute DRAM derivative is
targeted for Q3 '26, with qualification ramp through Q4 and volume
production in early 2027 contingent on wafer yield crossing the 80%
process window target.

## HBM

HBM4 controller RTL freeze in July; TSV stack qualification follows the
cache controller regression. Mask set release for the KESTREL-X reticle
is on hold pending the metal2 DRC deck waiver.

## NAND

3D NAND firmware (git/nand-fw) moves to the new sense amplifier design;
row hammer mitigation changes in the bank group arbiter increase refresh
interval pressure and are being characterized on the pilot line.
