Privacy note — pranaymokida.xyz
================================

This is a static personal portfolio. It uses no advertising and sets no
tracking cookies.

Visitor recon logging
---------------------
The site's hero "recon" widget reads your approximate location (via the
third-party service ipapi.co) and your device details to display a line back to
you. When central logging is enabled, the following technical details are
recorded to a private Google Sheet owned by the site author:

  - IP address and IP version
  - Approximate location from your IP: city, region, country, continent,
    postal code, latitude/longitude, timezone, currency, calling code
  - Network operator / ASN / organisation
  - Browser, operating system, platform, and full user-agent string
  - Browser language(s) and browser timezone
  - Screen size, viewport size, pixel ratio, colour depth
  - Approximate device memory, CPU core count, touch support
  - Network connection type and downlink speed
  - Cookie-enabled flag and Do-Not-Track setting
  - The referring URL, and the page/title you landed on
  - A timestamp

This data is collected to understand who visits the site. It is not sold or
shared. One entry is recorded per browser session.

Third party
-----------
Geolocation is provided by ipapi.co; their handling of your IP is governed by
their own policy (https://ipapi.co/privacy/).
