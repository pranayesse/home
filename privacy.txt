Privacy note — pranaymokida.xyz
================================

This is a static personal portfolio. It uses no advertising and sets no
tracking cookies.

Visitor recon logging
---------------------
The site's hero "recon" widget reads your approximate location (via the
third-party service ipapi.co) and your browser/OS to display a line back to
you. When central logging is enabled, the following is recorded to a private
Google Sheet owned by the site author:

  - IP address (and ISP/org, approximate city/region/country) from ipapi.co
  - Browser and operating system
  - Browser language, referring URL, and the page you landed on
  - A timestamp

This data is collected to understand who visits the site. It is not sold or
shared. One entry is recorded per browser session.

Third party
-----------
Geolocation is provided by ipapi.co; their handling of your IP is governed by
their own policy (https://ipapi.co/privacy/).
